About Us

आंभिरा इन्स्टिट्यूट ऑफ सायन्समध्ये आपले स्वागत आहे.

Welcome to Ambhira Institute of Science, a premier educational hub dedicated to empowering students to achieve academic excellence. With a passion for learning and a commitment to quality, we offer comprehensive online and offline classes for students of all ages.


आमच्या सर्विसेस:

* प्राथमिक ते माध्यमिक शिक्षण: 1ली ते 10वी

* उच्च माध्यमिक शिक्षण: 11वी आणि 12वी (सायन्स)

* स्पर्धात्मक परीक्षा तयारी: नवोदय, स्कॉलरशिप, NMMS, MTS

* इंजिनियरिंग आणि मेडिकल प्रवेश परीक्षांची तयारी: CET, JEE Main, NEET

Mission

Our mission is to provide personalized guidance and support to students, helping them unlock their full potential and succeed in an increasingly competitive world. We proudly stand as a beacon of educational excellence, shaping the minds of future leaders and change-makers.

Philosophy

Student-centric approach, focusing on individual needs and goals.
Foster a supportive community, encouraging collaboration and growth.
Emphasize conceptual clarity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.



Promise

Deliver high-quality education, accessible and affordable.
Provide personalized attention, guidance, and mentorship.
Empower students to achieve their academic goals and succeed in their chosen paths.


Our Expertise

Comprehensive preparation for competitive exams:
CAT, CBSE Board, Civil Services, CTET, GATE, JEE, NEET, SSC CGL, SSC Government, and UGC Net
Foundational learning for students from Class 1 to 12, fostering a strong academic base
Experienced instructors, proven methodologies, and engaging curriculum ensure holistic development
